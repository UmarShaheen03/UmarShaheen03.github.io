About

Who I am

I’m in my final year of a Bachelor of Advanced Computer Science at the University of Western Australia, majoring in International Cybersecurity. Over the past few years I’ve balanced deep technical work in AI audio watermarking and machine learning with hands-on experience in cybersecurity, software engineering, and leadership.

My Honours dissertation focuses on using model fingerprinting and watermarking to detect stolen or misused generative audio models. I build and analyse pipelines that embed robust watermarks into audio, fine-tune models like MusicGen, and evaluate how well those marks survive real-world attacks.

Outside of research, I’ve worked on projects ranging from agent-based simulations of the Australian housing market to Neo4j-backed family tree visualisations and gym and housing app concepts that take ideas from whiteboard to prototype.
